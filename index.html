About Me

I'm someone with many interests. Working as the director of cultivation for a large indoor cultivation center, my days are spent solving unique problems with variables that vary widely from day to day. Along with video and board games and creating music and art, I stay busy.

Spending time in the mountains is important to me and rock climbing is my sport. The teamwork keeps me responsible while pushing my mental and physical limits. I'm better for it.
